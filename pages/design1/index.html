WHAT TO DO?

QUESTS!

There are over 200+ quests to do! Make sure to finish the quests to earn unique rewards and level up! Some quests are also part of the story so we recommend not skipping any details.

FOODS!

Foods are very important in Slime Mastery as it is the most efficient and faster way to level up your slimes! You can buy food at a food shop, or get them by killing mobs. You can also sell food if you think you have too many in your storage.

FABRICATOR!

Upgrading your tools and equipments is very important as you level up! The fabricator will be responsible for upgrading your equipments so make sure to collect the right ingredients to successfully upgrade your equipments.
